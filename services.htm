Our Services

What We Offer

Football Development

Training programs, youth academies, and grassroots initiatives to grow football talent across Sierra Leone.

Leagues Management

Organizing national and regional leagues, including fixtures, results, and league standings.

National Teams

Support and management of all national football teams, including coaching, logistics, and match arrangements.

Referee Training

Certification programs and development courses for referees to maintain high officiating standards.

Community Engagement

Programs promoting football participation, social responsibility, and healthy lifestyles in communities.

Events & Competitions

Organizing football tournaments, cup competitions, and friendly matches to promote competitive play.

Sierra Leone FA

Promoting and supporting football across Sierra Leone — development, leagues, and national teams.
