AGENCY INTRODUCTION

The Sierra Leone Football Association (SLFA) is dedicated to developing, promoting, and elevating football across the nation. Our vision is to empower both youth and professional players through structured programs, transparent governance, and international collaboration. We manage all national teams, including the Leone Stars, and oversee the organization and running of the Sierra Leone Premier League and other domestic cup competitions. We are committed to fair play, inclusivity, and achieving global success.

**Mission Statement:** To Govern, Develop, and Promote Football for all citizens of Sierra Leone.
